    About Us

    We are an Audio Post And Music Production Studio for Songs, Films, Music, VO’s, Ads, Short Films etc…. We do Dubbing, Dub Matching, Live Foley, SFX, Background Music, Stereo and 5.1 Surround Mixing, Composing, Arrangement, Voice Overs, Film Previews, Cover Songs etc..

    We can step in, right at the pre production stage, from composing, recording and mixing your soundtrack in stereo for Cd publishing, to dubbing your film post edit, sound design and foley, background score and a 5.1 pre mix. Or you could just hire the facilities and work with your own creative team and engineers.

    History

    AD 1995 : Studio Octavius opens with completely revolutionary interiors, acoustics, attitude and ambience. Music directors like Ehsaan-Loy, Shiv Mathur, Louis Banks, Zubin, Ashley, all gravitate towards this breath of fresh air. And Octavius rules.

    Followed by periodic expansions and takeovers of other struggling studios, with the Octavius brand and expertise, six markedly different studios are established under the flagship Octavius. They are 32nd Street, Cubix, Sound Bar, Sound Byte, and Sound Den. Clients now have a variety of choice for price and location. And much less of listening to 'Sorry, we're booked'.

    In these 21 years, a lot of people have grown along with us. Many engineers have started their successful careers at Octavius. Most freelancers have had a fruitful stint there at some period of their careers. Some have even gone a step further and set up studios, obviously gaining more than just experience and engineering knowledge from their time at Octavius.
